Product care

1. To clean the device, use a soft and dry cloth.

2. Do not use paint thinners, solvents, cleaning fluids, or chemicals on the cloth.

3. Do not apply too much force on the On/Off button.

4. The piano is best stored in the following condition: 59-95 degrees fahrenheit, and a humidity level of 75%.
